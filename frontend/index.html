About Testnet

What is Testnet?
Binance Futures Testnet is a sandbox environment with fake USDT. All orders and balances are simulated — no real money involved.
Get Testnet Funds
Visit testnet.binancefuture.com → log in → click "Get 10,000 USDT" to fund your testnet account.
API Keys
Testnet API keys are separate from your real Binance account. Generate them at testnet.binancefuture.com.
